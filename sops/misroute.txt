STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE – MISROUTE

Purpose:
To correct situations where a package is sent to the wrong facility or placed on the wrong delivery route.

Procedure:

1. Identify Routing Error
   - GPS mismatch with destination ZIP/Postal Code.
   - Hub scan indicates incorrect region.

2. Immediate Reroute
   - Transfer package to correct outbound lane.
   - If misroute found on driver route, return to station same day.

3. Customer Communication
   - Notify: “Package temporarily delayed due to routing error.”

4. Internal Root Cause Analysis
   - Check label readability.
   - Examine sort sequence.
   - Identify manual scanning mistakes.

5. Resolution Timeline
   - Domestic: deliver within +1 business day.
   - International: may require customs resubmission.

6. Final Disposition
   - Mark as “Corrected Route” in system.
