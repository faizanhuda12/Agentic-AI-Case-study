STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE – WEATHER DELAY

Purpose:
To manage delays caused by unsafe weather conditions including snow, ice, storms, flooding, and extreme temperatures.

Procedure:

1. Safety Evaluation
   - Drivers must not operate in unsafe conditions.
   - Management confirms service suspension zones.

2. Package Handling
   - Retain packages at station until conditions improve.
   - Protect temperature-sensitive shipments.

3. Customer Notification
   - Automated message: “Delivery delayed due to severe weather.”

4. Resume Operations
   - Prioritize backlog once conditions clear.
   - Use recovery routes for high-volume areas.

5. Regulatory Compliance
   - Follow local emergency restrictions (e.g., road closures).

6. Final Disposition
   - Deliver next available day.
