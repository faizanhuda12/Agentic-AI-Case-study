STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE – SYSTEM ERROR

Purpose:
To handle failures caused by scanning errors, corrupted labels, duplicate tracking IDs, or system outages.

Procedure:

1. Identify Failure
   - Barcode unreadable
   - No scan event for > 24 hours
   - Duplicate tracking record detected

2. Re-scan Attempts
   - Try backup scanner.
   - Manually enter tracking ID.

3. IT Escalation
   - If issue persists, open ticket with ops IT team.
   - Attach photo of label.

4. Customer Update
   - Notify: “Package processing issue – resolution in progress.”

5. Recovery
   - Once tracking corrected, prioritize movement.

6. Security
   - Verify package identity before dispatch.
