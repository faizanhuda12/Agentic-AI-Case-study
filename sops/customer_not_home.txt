Purpose:
To manage failed delivery attempts when a signature is required or the customer is unavailable.

Procedure:

1. Verify Delivery Attempt
   - Driver must knock/ring doorbell.
   - Capture photo or leave door tag as proof of attempt.

2. Customer Notification
   - Automated SMS sent within 5 minutes.
   - Note whether signature is required.

3. Re-delivery Logic
   - Residential: up to 3 attempts permitted.
   - Commercial: up to 2 attempts permitted.
   - Attempt must occur on consecutive business days unless customer requests otherwise.

4. Station Hold Option
   - Customer may request hold for pickup.
   - Package held for 5 business days unless otherwise specified.

5. Return-to-Sender Policy
   - After 3 failed attempts (or 2 for commercial), return to shipper.

6. Safety and Security
   - Do not leave package unless driver receives explicit permission and release waiver is on file.
