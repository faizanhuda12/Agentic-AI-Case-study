STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE – DRIVER ISSUE

Purpose:
To handle exceptions caused by driver-related constraints such as fatigue, minor accidents, vehicle breakdown, distraction, or safety compliance.

Procedure:

1. Immediate Safety Check
   - Driver contacts dispatcher.
   - Confirm driver is safe and unharmed.
   - Remove driver from route if safety compromised.

2. Document Incident
   - Record reason: fatigue, illness, breakdown, police interaction, minor collision.
   - Log timestamp and GPS coordinates.

3. Route Continuity
   - Reassign undelivered packages to backup driver where possible.
   - If reassignment impossible, schedule next-day delivery.

4. Customer Notification
   - Automated message: “Unexpected operational delay – rescheduled.”

5. Compliance Review
   - If incident indicates a safety violation, escalate to fleet supervisor.

6. Final Disposition
   - Resume delivery only when driver is cleared to continue.
