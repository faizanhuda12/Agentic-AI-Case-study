Purpose:
To manage incidents where the package appears crushed, torn, wet, leaking, or compromised.

Procedure:

1. Assessment
   - Determine severity: Minor, Moderate, Critical.
   - Check for hazardous material leakage.

2. Customer Notification
   - For minor damage, attempt delivery with customer consent.
   - For major damage, hold at station for shipper review.

3. Documentation
   - Take photos of all sides.
   - Add incident note with timestamp.

4. Shipper Instructions
   - Contact shipper for handling preference:
       - Deliver as-is
       - Return to station
       - Return-to-sender
       - Dispose (if allowed)

5. Safety Protocol
   - For hazardous material leakage, activate HAZMAT SOP.

6. Final Disposition
   - Deliver or return based on shipper decision.
