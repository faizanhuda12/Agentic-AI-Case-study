Purpose:
To address situations where the cause of delivery failure cannot be determined.

Procedure:

1. Review Driver Notes
   - Confirm no contradictory information.
   - Contact driver for clarification.

2. System Cross-Check
   - Validate GPS logs.
   - Validate scan history.
   - Validate address metadata.

3. Customer Outreach
   - Request confirmation of availability and address.

4. Dispatch Review
   - Dispatcher chooses most reasonable attempt strategy.

5. Final Disposition
   - Schedule re-delivery next business day.
   - Flag package for manual monitoring.
